## Introduction
NINA is installed on the NUC.

## Workflow at the Observatory
1. Power everything up
2. Check all USB hub power lights are ON
3. Start Computer
4. Open the shutter
5. Remove the lens cap on C14
6. Initialise Sitech/ Scope at 270 degrees (Scope west and horizontal)
7. Run monitor software & connect
8. Check dome aperture aligns with scope & Azimuth reads 255 degrees.
9. Run Anydesk
